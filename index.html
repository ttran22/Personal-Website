About Me

I am currently a high school senior who is aspires to be a Software Engineer

  • Palo Duro High School, GPA: 101.54, Expected May 26th, 2022
  • Amarillo College, GPA: 4.0/4.0

A literal screaming Roomba led me to pursue my dreams. I originally thought coding was a dull task until I saw videos a drone being hacked using Scratch and a robot poorly performing surgery. I was inspired to learn to code. I became addicted to the satisfaction I felt after finishing a program and seeing it come to life. I looked forward to working on new projects. I want to code programs that can be used and enjoyed by everyone. I want to be a trailblazer in the programming field.
